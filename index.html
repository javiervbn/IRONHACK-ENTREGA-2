Welcome to the world of Bonsai Trees, an ancient art form combining horticulture and artistic styling that originated in Asia over a thousand years ago. Bonsai involves cultivating miniature trees that mimic the shape and scale of full-size trees. Here, you'll learn all about the different types of bonsai, the essential tools required for their care, and how to cultivate your own living masterpiece.
